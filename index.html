Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the rich history of Cleveland's fences. We believe that a city's character is not just in its buildings, but in the beautiful and varied fences that surround them. We are dedicated to the documentation, preservation, and appreciation of these often-overlooked works of art.

Our Charter

The Cleveland Steel Historical Fencing Society is a 501(c)(2) non-profit organization dedicated to the study and appreciation of historical fences in Cleveland, Ohio. We are committed to:

  • Documentation: Creating a comprehensive archive of Cleveland's historic fences, including photographs, historical records, and architectural details.
  • Preservation: Advocating for the protection and restoration of significant historical fences throughout the city.
  • Education: Raising public awareness about the artistic and historical importance of fences through workshops, tours, and online resources.

A Note on Fencing: It has come to our attention that some people may confuse our organization with the sport of fencing. To be clear, we think the sport of fencing is dumb.
